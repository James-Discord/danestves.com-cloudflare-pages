## What is Netnovation?

Professional experts in network technical support, installation of platforms under Open Source or Microsoft environment, licenses and support services for Zimbra, Endian and administration of your local or cloud servers.

## What problem was solved?

Netnovation is a company that has been in the technology market for more than 10 years offering hosting services and much more to its customers. The main problem was that they were using Voice to Voice Marketing as advertising, they created a website with which you can buy their products, manage them, customer service, a blog, a contact section and an online chat to contact support.

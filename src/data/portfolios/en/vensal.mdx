## What is Vensal?

22 years building alliances with its customers. Vensal produces first quality salt for industrial, animal and human consumption.

> _**A grain of salt in every Venezuelan industry and home**_. We started to commercialize salt for industrial use and positioned ourselves as an ally of the top 10 manufacturing industries in the country.

## What problem was solved?

This food company faced a very common problem in industries that have not gone digital: they had no online presence. With the creation of this website, Vensal's history, products and services, important allies with which it works and a smooth interface to draw the user's attention to learn more about their business.
